### How to Request an Account
1. Go to the ClearAI website
2. Click "Request Account" or go to `/request-account`

7. Check your email (including spam folder) for your password
8. Log in with your email and the password you received

## â“ Common Questions

### Q: I submitted a request, what now?
**A:** Wait for an email with your password. Check spam folder. Usually takes 1-2 business days.

### Q: I can't find the logout button
**A:** Click your profile picture/name in the top-right corner, then click "Sign Out" at the bottom of the dropdown.

### Q: Where do I approve requests as admin?
**A:** Log in â†’ Profile â†’ Admin Panel â†’ Account Requests section

### Q: Why don't I see approve/reject buttons in the email?
**A:** For security, you must log into the Admin Dashboard to approve/reject. The email is just a notification.

### Q: I didn't receive the password email
**A:** Check spam folder. If still not there, contact your administrator.

### Q: Can I change my password?
**A:** Yes, use the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.
